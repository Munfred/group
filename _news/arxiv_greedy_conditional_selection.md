[Stephen Huan's](https://cgdct.moe/) paper on greedy conditional selection of sparsity patterns for [KL optimal Cholesky factorization](https://epubs.siam.org/doi/abs/10.1137/20M1336254) now [on the arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.11648). 
Joint work with [Joe Guinness](https://stat.cornell.edu/people/faculty/joe-guinness), [Matthias Katzfuss](https://sites.google.com/view/katzfuss/), and [Houman Owhadi](http://users.cms.caltech.edu/~owhadi/).
Also check out Stephen's [blog post-style summary](https://cgdct.moe/projects/cholesky/) of the paper.
